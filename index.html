About Us

Scottish Public School established in 1991, functioned from a few tents pitched on the ground cleared from the onslaught of brambles. Today, spread over an area of about 3 acres of lush, green lawns, in the city of Katihar, Scottish Public School is a co-educational day-cum-boarding school. The foundation stone of the school building was laid in 1991 by Dr. Avinash. This school is under The Satyam Educational And Social Enhancement Trust.

From The Desk of Director

The journey of Scottish Public School has always been a path of ups and downs. We never lost our ideas during the time what we call as downs. It is needless to say that the school received unstated cooperation from both the teaching and non-teaching faculties.

Right from our very inception, we believe that if the little learners are given scope, they can develop new innovative ideas and can involve themselves in the ocean of knowledge through the information passed on to them by the educators of the school. Our school has always focused on to encourage the learners to have a strong base on the subject matter and to reflect their own ideas through practical activities. We never trusted on rote learning process.

We believe that knowledge can be acquired by taking a dip into the lectures given by the teachers in the classroom, getting the light of knowledge outside the classroom by engaging themselves in some different and imaginary activities. We continuously work on making the teaching learning process to be stress free, interesting and activity based so that the students can relate the study materials with day-to-day things. We try to make the learning process to be remembered as a happy experience for all. In the New Education Policy 2020, the same has been acknowledged by the learned educators and the Government of the country. We are also getting to the tune in with National Education Policy 2020.

We know that the students of this new generation are very sensitive and ought to be eyed with extreme care. We try to get into the touch of each and every student and with their parents by making a telephone call or by sending SMS or to meet them physically during the Parents Teachers Meeting (PTM) or in any other events. One to one conversation with our prudent guardians helps us a lot to furnish our jobs easily.

School House

Saket House

The colour green represents abundance, renewal, growth and nature. Green is healing colour that gives healing energy to the heart. Therefore the students who represent our Green House express unconditional love, forgiveness and compassion.

Takshashila House

The yellow colour represents happiness, clarity, fulfilment, abundance and self confidence. This colour always stands for wisdom and intellect throughout the ages. Therefore those who represent our Yellow House are aided with logic, memory, will power and concentration.

Nalanda House

The colour red represents power, energy, vitality, dominance, action, assertion, creation, survival and passion. Therefore those who represent our Red House they are always ready to take action on a new goal or project that one that had been putting off.

Vikramshila House

The colour blue represents deep colour, often associated with stability, honesty, truth and tranquillity. Therefore those who represent our Blue House they have universal associations and their own perception of the world, how it appears to be.
